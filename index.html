Executive Overview

Financial Planning & Analysis at Vanguard

FP&A aligns strategic priorities to financial outcomes through integrated planning, rolling forecasts, governance discipline, and concise executive decision support.

Strategic clarity: Resources focused on highest-value initiatives.

Forward visibility: Risk and performance signals identified early.

Decision velocity: Leadership receives clear actions, not just data.

FP&A Mandate

Strategy Translation

Convert enterprise objectives into measurable revenue, cost, capital, and productivity commitments.

Performance Stewardship

Monitor growth, efficiency, and client outcomes with balanced KPIs and consistent accountability.

Capital Discipline

Evaluate investments through transparent business cases, stage gates, and benefits realization.

Planning & Forecasting Cycle

Long-Range Plan (3-5 Years)

Define multi-year trajectory by business segment with strategic assumptions and target envelopes.

Annual Operating Plan

Set annual commitments with quarterly phasing, ownership, and measurable execution priorities.

Rolling Reforecast

Refresh full-year outlooks using key drivers including markets, flows, pricing, and productivity.

Variance-to-Action

Quantify root causes, assess implications, and assign corrective actions with explicit timelines.

Executive Reporting & Analytics

KPI Architecture

Balanced scorecard of growth, cost efficiency, client outcomes, and risk-adjusted performance.

Leadership Reviews

Monthly review packs with trend context, outlook changes, and decisions required.

Scenario Insights

Base, upside, and downside sensitivity analysis to support resilient trade-off decisions.

Governance & Risk Integration

  • Investment governance: Standardized checkpoints from concept to delivery.
  • Control rigor: Auditable assumptions, model governance, and consistent data definitions.
  • Scenario readiness: Practical contingency views for changing market and business conditions.
  • Outcome tracking: Benefit realization monitored against approved business cases.

Operating Model

Business FP&A Partners

Embed with divisions to shape plans and challenge assumptions near execution.

Corporate FP&A

Consolidate enterprise views and guide strategic trade-off discussions with leadership.

Analytics & Systems

Increase forecast accuracy and cycle speed via automation, data quality, and dashboards.

Monthly Cadence

Close → Analyze → Reforecast → Review → Act, with named owners and tracked actions.
